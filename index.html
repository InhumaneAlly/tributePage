Fsociety, initially forged and crafted by Elliot himself is first seen to be the masterpiece of his late father who happens to be only visible to Elliot from time to time due to the former's condition in psychological well-being. A loner, a high functioning introvert with mood swings and inexplicable disorders only limited by the imagination of the narrator which is Elliot himself.
